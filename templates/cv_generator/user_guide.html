Building Your CV

Two Methods Available: You can either upload an existing CV for AI processing or start from scratch with manual entry.

Method 1: Upload & AI Processing

1Access CV Builder Pro

Click on "CV Builder Pro" from the main navigation or dashboard. This is our advanced CV builder with AI capabilities.

2Upload Your CV

In the "Upload & Parse Your CV" section:

  • Click "Choose File" and select your CV
  • Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT
  • Maximum file size: 10MB
  • Click "Parse CV & Auto-Fill"
Supported Formats
PDF
DOCX
DOC
TXT
Important: Ensure your CV has clear structure and readable text. Avoid complex layouts or text in images.
3Review AI Extraction

After processing, the AI will automatically populate all CV sections. Review each section and make adjustments as needed:

  • Personal information and contact details
  • Professional summary
  • Work experience with dates and descriptions
  • Education and qualifications
  • Skills and competencies
  • Additional sections (projects, certifications, etc.)

Method 2: Manual Entry

1Start Fresh

If you prefer to build your CV from scratch or don't have an existing CV, skip the upload section and scroll down to the CV sections.

2Fill Each Section

Complete each section systematically. Use the "Add" buttons to create multiple entries for experience, education, etc.

CV Sections Guide

Personal Information
  • • Full name and professional title
  • • Phone number and email
  • • Address (city/country is sufficient)
  • • LinkedIn and portfolio URLs
Professional Summary
  • • 2-3 sentences describing your expertise
  • • Key achievements and skills
  • • Career goals and aspirations
  • • Industry-specific keywords
Work Experience
  • • Job title and company name
  • • Employment dates (start and end)
  • • Key responsibilities and achievements
  • • Quantify results where possible
Education
  • • Degree/qualification title
  • • Institution name and location
  • • Graduation date or expected date
  • • Relevant coursework or achievements
Pro Tip: After filling out basic information, use the "Reset & Add Manually" button if you want to switch from auto-filled to manual entry.

Troubleshooting & Support

Common Issues & Solutions

File Size Issues
  • Ensure file is under 10MB
  • Try compressing large PDF files
  • Convert images to text-based formats
Format Problems
  • Use standard fonts (Arial, Times New Roman, Calibri)
  • Avoid complex layouts or graphics
  • Ensure text is selectable, not embedded in images
  • Try saving as a different format (DOCX to PDF)

Slow Processing
  • Wait up to 30 seconds for complex analyses
  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Use shorter job descriptions for faster processing
Inaccurate Results
  • Ensure job description is complete and detailed
  • Use the full job posting, not just a summary
  • Check that your CV information is accurate
  • Try rewording unclear job requirements

Preview Not Loading
  • Enable pop-ups for this website
  • Try using Chrome or Firefox browsers
  • Disable ad blockers temporarily
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
Formatting Issues
  • Ensure all required fields are filled
  • Check for special characters in your text
  • Try a different template
  • Contact support if issues persist

PDF Generation Fails
  • Check that all required sections are completed
  • Try a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions
  • Wait for page to fully load before exporting
Download Issues
  • Check your browser's download settings
  • Ensure sufficient storage space
  • Try right-clicking and "Save as"
  • Contact support for persistent issues
